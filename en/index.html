Common questions about Factor0x

Investors finance verified B2B invoices. Each deal has an amount, term, risk tier, verification status, and an expected repayment process.

Yield is formed after the debtor repays the invoice. When the business's client pays, funds are distributed among deal participants according to the agreed terms.
